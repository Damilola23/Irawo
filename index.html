About Us

Irawọ was birthed for the sole purpose of providing solution to the issue of quality education.

This intiative empowers mothers(low income earners) through adult night school education with a compulsory stipulation of registering their wards/children for online courses.

Irawọ is an educational platform for mothers and children which is tailored to resolve SDG goal 4(quality education) in Nigeria.
